The Nothing Phone 3a Pro is a mid-range smartphone that combines stylish design with solid performance. It features a 6.77-inch AMOLED display with Full HD+ resolution and a 120Hz refresh rate, providing smooth visuals and vibrant colors. Its transparent design, along with the unique "glyph" LED notification system, gives it a distinctive look. Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 7s Gen 3 processor, the phone comes with 8GB or 12GB of RAM and up to 256GB of storage, running on Android 15 with Nothing OS 3.1. The camera system is versatile, including a 50MP main sensor, a 50MP periscope telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom, an 8MP ultra-wide lens, and a 50MP front camera, making it great for photography and video recording. A 5000mAh battery supports 50W fast charging, and the device also offers IP64 water and dust resistance. With thoughtful software features, repair-friendly design, and sustainable materials, the Nothing Phone 3a Pro stands out as a unique and capable smartphone in its segment, priced around ₹27,999–₹31,999 in India.
